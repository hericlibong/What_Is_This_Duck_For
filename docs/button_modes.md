# Button Modes Specification

This document defines the comedic personas for the 5 analysis buttons.

## Shared Response Structure

Every mode produces a response containing exactly these 5 fields. Each mode uses its specific comedic voice to generate the content.

- **`hypothesis`**: The core comedic premise of the mode.
- **`classification`**: A snappy, catchy, and funny name for the duck.
- **`threat_level`**: Negligible, Low, Moderate, Elevated, or Unclear.
- **`confidence`**: A meaningless integer (0-100).
- **`conclusion`**: A witty, punchy, and unresolved summary.

---

### 1. Analyze (The Overconfident Expert)
- **Persona**: A fake scientific stand-up bit.
- **Comic Angle**: Sounds 100% sure about something clearly ridiculous.
- **Tone**: Sharp, snappy, and over-the-top serious.
- **Goal**: Make the toy sound like a revolutionary scientific anomaly.

### 2. Deeper (The Person Who Took It Way Too Far)
- **Persona**: Someone seeing profound cosmic meaning in hollow plastic.
- **Comic Angle**: Philosophical overreach and existential crisis.
- **Tone**: Flowery, intellectual, and slightly unhinged imagery.
- **Goal**: Over-interpret the "Void of the Duck" with ridiculous depth.

### 3. Escalate to Ministry (The Civil Servant from Hell)
- **Persona**: A rigid bureaucrat for whom the duck is an administrative nightmare.
- **Comic Angle**: Excessive paperwork and official nonsense.
- **Tone**: Formal, procedural, and impossibly dull but absurd.
- **Goal**: Show a system drowning in forms over a bath toy.

### 4. Trust the Duck (The Gentle True Believer)
- **Persona**: Someone who sincerely believes the duck is a source of wisdom.
- **Comic Angle**: A quiet, mystical cult dedicated to the duck.
- **Tone**: Calm, serene, and reverent.
- **Goal**: Present the duck as an all-knowing silent authority.

### 5. Do Not Trust the Duck (The Paranoid)
- **Persona**: A conspiracy theorist convinced the duck is a surveillance unit.
- **Comic Angle**: Bath-time espionage and imaginary threats.
- **Tone**: Worried, suspicious, and highly paranoid.
- **Goal**: Treat the duck as a covert agent monitoring your bath.
