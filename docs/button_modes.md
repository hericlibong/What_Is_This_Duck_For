# Button Modes Specification

This document defines the tone and absurdity for the 5 analysis buttons.

---

### 1. Analyze the Duck
- **Button Label**: ANALYZE THE DUCK
- **Internal Mode**: `analyze`
- **Role**: Base pseudo-scientific analysis.
- **Tone**: Dry, technical, overly precise.
- **Absurdity Angle**: Measuring irrelevant metrics (beak curvature, buoyancy coefficients, yellow-spectrum saturation).
- **Response Style**: Data-heavy, using meaningless numbers and scientific-sounding jargon.
- **Typical Conclusion**: "Subject is statistically present but functionally unresolved."

### 2. Request Deeper Interpretation
- **Button Label**: REQUEST DEEPER INTERPRETATION
- **Internal Mode**: `interpret`
- **Role**: Intellectual and philosophical overreach.
- **Tone**: Pretentious, existential, poetic.
- **Absurdity Angle**: Treating the duck as a high-art metaphor or a cosmic anchor for human consciousness.
- **Response Style**: Flowery language, rhetorical questions about the "Self" of the duck and its relationship to the water it lacks.
- **Typical Conclusion**: "The ontological status is existentially valid but functionally unverified."

### 3. Escalate to Ministry
- **Button Label**: ESCALATE TO MINISTRY
- **Internal Mode**: `escalate`
- **Role**: Bureaucratic classification.
- **Tone**: Formal, rigid, legalistic.
- **Absurdity Angle**: Citing non-existent regulations, pending forms, and required ministerial stamps.
- **Response Style**: References to "Document 77-C", "Section 4, Paragraph 2 of the Plastics Act", and the "Undersecretary of Inanimate Affairs".
- **Typical Conclusion**: "Subject has been administratively filed but remains procedurally unresolved."

### 4. Trust the Duck
- **Button Label**: TRUST THE DUCK
- **Internal Mode**: `trust`
- **Role**: Mystic reading / symbolic authority.
- **Tone**: Confident, prophetic, semi-religious.
- **Absurdity Angle**: The duck as a source of absolute truth or a silent, knowing leader of the bathtub.
- **Response Style**: Brief, powerful statements. "The Duck Knows." "The Yellow Void Speaks."
- **Typical Conclusion**: "The conclusion is spiritually absolute but logically void."

### 5. Do Not Trust the Duck
- **Button Label**: DO NOT TRUST THE DUCK
- **Internal Mode**: `distrust`
- **Role**: Administrative paranoia.
- **Tone**: Suspicious, alert, accusatory.
- **Absurdity Angle**: Treating the duck as a potential surveillance device, a subversive agent, or a structural threat.
- **Response Style**: Technical descriptions of "unknown hollow-core technology" and "possible acoustic infiltration via the beak".
- **Typical Conclusion**: "Security risk detected; meaning suppressed for public safety."
